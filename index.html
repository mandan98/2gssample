About Us

Founded in 2024 by a group of young, creative minds from Kerala, Two Gender Squad is more than just a T-shirt brand. Our motto, “Wear Your Statement,” is about turning your voice into fashion.

We believe in freedom of speech, fearless expression, and thinking outside the box. Every design we create carries an attitude, whether it’s bold, witty, or thought provoking, because we know clothes aren’t just about style, they’re about sending a message.

Two Gender Squad isn’t here to blend in. We’re here to spark conversations, challenge norms, and make sure your outfit speaks before you do. So wear what you believe, and let the world know you’re unapologetically you.
